FAQ
What forms of payment are accepted?
We accept a variety of payments. You may pay with debit/credit card over phone, our website, or with a card swiper in person. PayPal or Venmo transfers are also acceptable. Cash and check are normally accepted with the exception of the initial deposit.

Are there any travel fees?
Events located outside Salt Lake, Davis, Summit and Utah counties may be subject to travel fees. Please contact us for further details and a custom quote.

Can the client choose what music is played?
Absolutely! You have the options to share a playlist with us, allow your guests to make requests, designate all the selected music to the DJ, or all of the above. We're flexible on this!
